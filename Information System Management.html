Admission Terms and Rules

Requirements for Acceptance :

Department of Information systems:


. Certificate of General Secondary Education (GSE) or an equivalent from another Arab country.
· Trading Diploma (3-5 years).
· Manufacturing Diploma (3-5 years) .
· Diploma in Administrative Services (3-5 years).
· Certificate of Secondary Education from Al-Azhar.
· Diploma from manufacturing institutes.
· Diploma in Computer.



The following qualifications are needed for acceptance in the second academic year :

· Diploma in Computer with a specialization in Administrative Information Systems, with minimum of 60% grade.
· Diploma from the Finance Department of Business Institutes with minimum of 60% grade.
· Diploma from the Information Systems or Computer Science Department of Business Institutes with minimum of 60% grade.



The following qualifications are needed for acceptance in the third academic year:

· Diploma in Computer with a specialization in Management information systems, with minimum of 65% grade.
