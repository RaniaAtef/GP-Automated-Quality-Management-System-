Admission Terms and Rules

Requirements for Acceptance :

Department of Business Administration and Accounting :


. Certificate of General Secondary Education (GSE) or an equivalent from another Arab country.
· Trading Diploma (3-5 years).
· Manufacturing Diploma (3-5 years) .
· Diploma in Administrative Services (3-5 years).
· Certificate of Secondary Education from Al-Azhar.
· Diploma from manufacturing institutes.
· Diploma in Computer.



The following qualifications are needed for acceptance in the second academic year :

· Diploma from business institutes.
· Diploma from administrative or secretarial institutes.
· Certificate of Secondary Education in Business (5 years).
